Pizza

A Great Pizza Recipe To Remeber.

A pretzel dough may not be your first thought when you want to make a pizza, but it's the perfect way to try something new.

And if you are both a fan of soft pretzels and pizza, you have come to the right place.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups warm water (about 110 degrees F (43 degrees C))
  • 1 ½ tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 (1/4-ounce) envelope active dry yeast
  • 3 ½ cups bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • neutral cooking oil, for greasing bowl
  • cooking spray
  • tablespoon baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons hot water
  • 2 cups shredded low-moisture, part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 30 pepperoni slices (such as Hormel)
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, melted
  • pretzel salt
  • 1 pack of mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Prepare the pizza
  3. Add flour and salt
  4. Shape dough into a ball
  5. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (230 degrees C)
  6. Combine baking soda and hot water
  7. Bake crust on pizza stone
  8. SPread cheese evenly over crust
  9. Bake in the oven at 425 degrees F (230 degrees C)
  10. Slice and serve after baking
